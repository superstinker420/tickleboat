Build Process of an Electric Skateboard

Research/Inspiration

Before starting the build process, I did some research and looked for inspiration on various websites and forums. I also decided on the design and specifications for my electric skateboard.

Battery Build

The battery is the most important part of an electric skateboard, so I decided to build my own using 18650 lithium-ion cells. I followed a tutorial online to assemble the battery pack and added a battery management system for safety.

Electronics

After building the battery, I started working on the electronics. I used an ESC (Electronic Speed Controller) and a motor to control the speed of the skateboard. I also added a remote control for easy operation.

Putting Everything Together

Finally, I assembled all the parts together to complete the electric skateboard. I made sure all the connections were secure and tested the skateboard before riding it for the first time.
